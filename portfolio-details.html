Key Services & Benefits

Smart Inventory

Tracks stock levels in real time by brand, bottle size, and category (wines, spirits, beer). Automatically alerts when stock is low, helping prevent losses and out-of-stock situations.

Pricing Control

Allows flexible pricing, discounts, and offers per product or category. Ideal for happy hours, promotions, and seasonal pricing without manual calculations.

AI-Enabled Smart POS

An intelligent point-of-sale system that uses AI to learn from sales patterns and stock movement. The system automatically identifies fast-moving products, slow-moving stock, and unusual sales activity to help reduce losses and improve profitability. Smart recommendations support better pricing, restocking, and promotional decisions without manual analysis.

AI Data Analysis & Management Insights

A built-in data analysis tool that converts raw sales data into clear, actionable insights for management. The system provides automated summaries on revenue trends, peak sales hours, best-selling brands, and stock performance, presented in easy-to-read dashboards.

Business Insights

Generates daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports showing top-selling brands, revenue trends, and staff performance. Supports better decision-making and profit tracking.

Staff Management

Controls staff access levels and tracks transactions per cashier. Improves accountability and reduces shrinkage and unauthorized discounts.
